About La Price Inc

La Price Inc is a Calgary-based healthcare provider, offering comprehensive services for individuals with disabilities or those going through difficult times. Our mission is to ensure that every person receives the quality care they deserve, helping them improve their quality of life and health.

We specialize in providing personalized services that focus on the needs of our patients, ensuring they receive the care and attention necessary to live comfortably and with dignity.
